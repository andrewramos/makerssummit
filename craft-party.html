Join the Party

The Best Craft Party Ever is a separate event designed simply to celebrate the fun of making and meeting other makers. You don't have to attend the conference to participate or be naturally crafty or artistically inclined. We've designed the party so everyone — professional and newbie alike — can try their hand at making something new.

Tickets are $50 per person (separate from the conference). The evening includes a full dinner provided by a local chef, access to DIY stations, and supplies for making.
