About this year's festival

The party begins at 7:00 PM on March 1, and you don't have to attend the conference to participate. The Best Craft Party Ever is a separate event designed simply to celebrate the fun of making and meeting other makers. You also don't have to be naturally crafty or artistically inclined to join us. We've designed the party so everyone — professional and newbie alike — can try their hand at something new.

This is the perfect time to learn something new or simply try your hand at a craft you've always wanted to make. Tickets are $50 per person (separate from the conference) and include a complete dinner, craft supplies, access to letterpress and screen printing maker stations, plus the chance to make new friends who also love handmade.
